Project Reflection

Creative Choices and Process: In this version of the "Choose Your Adventure" game, the focus was on creating a thrilling and eerie experience. Each stage of the story is accompanied by atmospheric images that enhance the feeling of being in a haunted mansion. The design aims to immerse the player in a spooky narrative.

Challenges and Solutions: A major challenge was creating a seamless narrative that maintains suspense throughout. By carefully planning the story structure and using evocative imagery, we achieved this. Ensuring the game is responsive on all devices was another challenge, which was addressed through flexible design and testing.

Insights and Learnings: This project emphasized the importance of atmosphere in storytelling. Visual and textual elements must work together to create an engaging experience. We also learned that providing clear and meaningful choices enhances player engagement and immersion.
